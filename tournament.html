Mission Statment

Understanding that high school students across the state of Michigan are scared of public speaking, there is a need for a place that challenges these barriers. Our Ann Arbor campus is the perfect spot to host this platform as it sees potential in all students.

  • Question: Where will our results be displayed?

    • Answer: They will be displayed through Tabroom.

  • Question: How should I dress?

    • Answer: Whatever fits your speaking event.
